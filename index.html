what's new

  • Slithering heard in walls late a night in St.Xaviers school

    There have recently been reports from students that they have been hearing slithering in the walls of the school, like a snake. Several kids have reported about this mystery, but no person can ever solve it. This is a big problem for the students, because they will not focus on their class because they will be thinking about the slithering in the walls. The teachers have to find a way to get the students to focus on their class, and they have to tell the students to remain calm and focus on their studies.

  • New remodeling of school cafeteria

    St.Xaviers school is looking to remodel their old cafeteria into something more modern which the students will like. The are taking votes from the students, asking them what the want the new remodeling to look like. Most of the students are into something modern, and theat was the final choice. Today, St.Xaviers school will start remodeling the cafeteria to make better and more modern, as per the students choice.
